Classes

  • Beach Yoga

    Sink your toes into the warm sands as our experienced instructors guide you through a gentle and invigorating yoga practice against the backdrop of the endless horizon.

  • Yoga Philosophy Lectures

    These lectures offer a gateway to a more profound understanding of the holistic philosophy that has inspired and guided yogis for centuries.

  • Mindful Yoga

    Rooted in the principles of mindfulness, these sessions offer a holistic approach to yoga, integrating breath, movement, and meditation.

  • Yoga Teacher Training Intensive

    This immersive program is designed for passionate individuals aspiring to become certified yoga instructors.

  • Sound Bath Yoga

    The union of meditative sound and intentional movement creates a transformative experience, fostering deep relaxation, heightened awareness, and a profound sense of inner balance.

  • Hike & Yoga

    This unique class offers the perfect blend of cardiovascular exercise, mindful movement, and deep relaxation, providing a holistic wellness experience in the heart of nature.
